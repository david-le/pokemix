What's Poke?

Think: sushi meets the the burrito bowl

A classic Hawaiian preparation, poke (pronounced poh-kay) is a salad of cubed raw fish marinated in sesame oil or soy sauce. Though traditionally made from ahi (yellowfin tuna), salmon or tofu are common substitutions, and the dish can be eaten on its own, or over white rice, noodles, or even spiraled vegetables. In other words: customization is the name of the game.

- RealSimple.com

Our Story

Founded in 2017, Poke Mix was created by our founders who love and and enjoyed delicious foods from all over the world. Our goal was to bring the great taste of an Hawaiian dish to Wichita, KS. Inspired by fresh, sustainable, and natural ingredients, we ensure that our seafood is the freshest of quality.

We strive to give Wichita cuisines from all over the world at a great price and within a great atmosphere. All are welcome partake in this great island favorite.
